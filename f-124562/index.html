Air Strike WW2
Description
Take to the skies and defend your kingdom! Air Strike: WWII is a realistic 3D fight simulator where you fly a WWII fighter plane and shoot down enemy aircraft. You have both guns and missiles to help you destroy the incoming fighters. You can use the power boost button to increase your speed. You can easily invert your controls for different handling.
Instructions
Arrows/WASD - Control plane Left Ctrl/Left Mouse - Fire X/Right click - Change weapon Space - Speed boost C/Middle Mouse button - Change view
Developer: Air Strike WW2, Category: 3D, Distributed by Free Online Games
