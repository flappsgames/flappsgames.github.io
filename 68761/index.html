Chain Connect
Description
Have fun, relax and forget the daily stress! Play this relaxing chain reaction game with a twist. Immerse and have fun!
Instructions
1. Hit the shining dots with particles 2. Click on a dot to start a chain 3. The dot explodes and particles fly around 4. The particles move towards the MOUSE 5. If you clear the top layer, you move to the layer beneath 6. Relax and have fun :)
