Urban Micro Racers
Description
Racing just got a little more... micro. Using the arrow keys to navigate the beautiful retro graphics, race against others in this crisp, top-down driving game. Grab power-ups like the turbo boost, health packs, and extra petrol to help you conquer the streets. Play through a ton of levels and pick from plenty of different cars (once you've unlocked them, of course).
Instructions
Arrow keys to drive.
