Caveman Run
Description
You play as Rehman, a caveman who lived 65 million years ago in the Cretaceous period. Use your stonezooka to survive in this lethal world in a fun side-scrolling adventure. You have unlimited amounts of stones so keep firing at the dinosaurs! Be alert and try to collect power-ups as you go, make sure you jump over the boulders!
