Sea Food And Shoot It
Description
The ocean is filled with grotesque sea creatures, and now you can finally clean it up! In this action-shooter, you'll take out the various sea creatures, from basic fish to stingrays and even octopuses. Getting a hit off one of the more elusive and rare creatures will give you additional bullets and time!
Instructions
Aim and shoot - Use the mouse
