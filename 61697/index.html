Splitty Adventures 2
Description
Help little Splitties gather the Sacred Fire and save the universe from the Evil Grenades. Discover balance between black and white with new possibilities.
Instructions
Just hit spacebar to split Splitty and try to survive long enough to collect all the fire! There is a limit - how much heroes alive at moment - you cant split if your population above it.
