Canyon Valley Rally
Description
Jeeps are designed for off-road challenges like this one, but that doesn't mean it'll be easy! Just staying upright will be tough in this intense desert race. Keep your balance, hug the turns, and knock into your opponents to flip them over and give yourself an edge. Incredible 3D graphics make this game easy to look at, but not easy to beat!
Instructions
Arrow keys to drive P to Pause Escape to quit to main menu
