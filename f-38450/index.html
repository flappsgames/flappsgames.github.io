Carrot Fantasy
Description
Carrot Fantasy is an insanely funny tower defence game, containing an amazing twenty-four levels plus extra bonus levels! Protect your carrots by any means possible - even if you have to create a wall of poop towers! Shoot the clouds to give you more space to place your weapons. Sometimes clearing an area will also give you a free weapon to use to defend your carrot. You can always use your FOG coins to buy or upgrade your weapons if you get stuck. Upgrading your weapons will increase both the strength of the weapon, but also its range. With two different modes of play, Carrot fantasy is sure to keep you engrossed for hours!
Instructions
Use the mouse cursor to position and place your defenses.
