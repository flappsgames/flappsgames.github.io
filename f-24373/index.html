Parkour
Description
Parkour is a Free-Running Game with graffiti added in. Escape from the police as you leap over vehicles, rooftops and other obstacles and collect aerosol cans of paint. The more jumps you do, the more sprays you get and the longer you go can avoid being caught, the more your score will build. Don't worry about falling too far as you will not be hurt.
Instructions
Run with the arrow keys, spacebar to jump and S to spray.
