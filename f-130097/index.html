Fire Truck City Driving Sim
Description
Fire Truck City Driving Simulator gives you the chance to drive a true to life Fire Truck around a deserted city. There is no one to slow you down and no fire to worry about. Practice your route knowledge skills, so if there was a fire you'd be the first crew there and a hero. If you crash and wreck your fire truck engine, don't worry a quick reset will have your truck all shiny and new again.... Ready for you to smash it up again. Don't forget to use the different camera angles to give you either the real behind the wheel feeling or a cool view.
Instructions
Arrows to drive. Space to handbrake
