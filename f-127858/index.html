Holo Golf
Description
Holo Golf is a fun fantasy packed mini golf game set in a space ship. With 20 challenging levels to keep you busy while you fly through space you start with some basic puts to get the hang of the game but move on to more challenging mini golf holes. Watch out for holes in the course, spinners, spring boards and crazy gaps. It's a real challenge to finish the game but still fun of course. Mini Golf really never has been so fun, Holo Golf combines the classic elements of play with easy to use touch controls, stunning graphics and some truly unique level design which will make your jaw drop, or at least take a few extra shots anyway.
Instructions
Use mouse to aim Left click to shoot Space bar to aim at the flag
