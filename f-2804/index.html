Keep Ups 2
Description
Keep Ups 2 is a simple yet highly addictive Football Game that will keep you busy for a long time. The aim is to try and keep a soccer ball in the air for as long as you can. Kick the ball up and prevent it from falling on to the ground. For extra fun, you should invite your friends to play the game and see who can get the highest score.
