Hanna in a Choppa
Description
Can you pilot Hanna and her choppa through 21 unique levels? Only you can help her rescue people at sea, herd sheep, navigate inside a cement mixer, give a giant a haircut and more!
Instructions
Arrow keys to fly, spacebar to deploy/retract winch. Roll with Z and X. Struggling to beat a level? Check out the official walkthrough: Part 1: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FO4NXjJqeOw Part 2: http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TdYIy8UCS-o
