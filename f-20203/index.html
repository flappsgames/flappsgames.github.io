School Bus License
Description
Getting your Commercial Driver License has never been more fun! In 25 driving levels, you'll be driving with a test instructor who will be monitoring your every move. The first lesson is a simple drive and park. The next levels can be a bit tricky to get right, so patience and careful driving will win out in the end. Use the keyboard to navigate, and get that CDL for the kid's sake!
