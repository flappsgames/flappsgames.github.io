Fart Blaster
Description
Fart Blaster is a funny Skill Game. The aim of the game is to fart without getting caught. You do this by hiding your farting noises amongst the normal sounds of passing by cars, trucks and busses. You have to try and not get caught for as long as possible. But if you get caught or you dont't release your inner gasses quick enough you face some very funny endings. Good Luck!!
