Counter Specialist
Description
In Counter Specialist, war has erupted, and in-between the massive fights, there are small squabbles that need to be dealt with. Using your mouse and keyboard, you'll need to fight off terrorists, defuse bombs, rescue hostages and more! Hint: E defuses a bomb and SPACE-BAR reloads. Switch your guns with Q. Complete the objectives, or die trying!
Instructions
Move - W,A,S,D keys Attack/shoot - Use the mouse Switch weapons - 1-4 number keys Defuse bomb/release hostage - E key Quick swap - Q key Reload - Spacebar Show map - X key
