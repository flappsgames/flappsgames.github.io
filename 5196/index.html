MAD: Mutually Assured Destruction
Description
Enemy missiles are attacking your base! Shoot them down and see how long you can last against the onslaught. Update: Due to popular demand, "Menu" and "Restart" buttons have been added to the in game buy screen, and screen shake has been reduced. Thanks for the feedback!
Instructions
Click to fire a missile. Try to aim ahead of an incoming missile for best effect. Destroying missiles gives you resources which can be used to purchase upgrades.
