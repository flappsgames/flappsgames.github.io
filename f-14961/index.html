Stalingrad 2
Description
In Stalingrad 2, the objective of the game is to balance your base so that you will have enough resources to buy both defenses and units to watch your base. In Stalingrad 2 you can build money factories along with factories for weapons and tanks . Its up to you to strategise for the best defense you can afford.
