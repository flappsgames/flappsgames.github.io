Air Strike Dog Fight
Description
Take to the skies in your Jet fighter plane and engage in dogfight warfare in this 3D jet pilot dogfight flight simulator. Enemies are flying all around you trying to blow you out of the sky, fly around them at high speeds and shoot them down before they shoot you! Switch weapons from sidewinders, missiles, carpet bombs and machine guns to destroy the opposing enemy jets and show you have air superiority. Be the master of the skies and the most skilled jet pilot ever to engage in a dogfight.
Instructions
Left Ctrl/Left mouse - Fire C/Middle Mouse button - Change camera Space - Speed boost X/Right Mouse - Change Weapon
