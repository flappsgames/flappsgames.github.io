Material Mole 2
Description
Material Mole is back! Can you deliver all the loads before time runs out? Get in your truck and carry everything to its destination as quickly as you can! Drop the items on your way, switch tracks to speed up your trip and collect all the hidden gems and items you find in your path with your trolley cart! Can you find them all?
Instructions
Use the arrow keys to drive Shift to drop hidden items Space to move ramps and barriers Z to use a speed boost
