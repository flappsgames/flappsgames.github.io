The Boomlands: World Wars
Description
The Boomlands: World Wars is an interesting twist on the strategy genre! Gather resources and guide your men to battle, to take over the opposing king's castle! Watch out though, they're trying to do the same to your king, and you've got to think fast to break through. The key is to build up a good rhythm between gathering resources, deploying soldiers, and allowing your resources to grow back!
Instructions
Select troops - Use mouse cursor
