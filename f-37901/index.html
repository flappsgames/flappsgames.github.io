Eat My Axe
Description
In Eat My Axe you must help the love-struck axeman rescue the princess from the evil mob, the only way to free her is by using your axe throwing skills to kill the monsters. Throw with the utmost of care and accuracy though as you really don't want to hit the princess! Aim straight, bounce off walls and destroy objects such as walls and TNT as you enjoy this fantastic physics game.
Instructions
Use the mouse to aim and left click to throw the axe.
