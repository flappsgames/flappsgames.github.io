Conquer Antarctica
Description
In Conquer Antarctica you take charge of a team of penguins. The aim is to battle other penguins by shooting snowballs, grenades and rocket launchers at them. Just like you the other teams will be standing on ice bergs and your aim to either knock them off or kill them. You can only proceed to the next battle if you win.
