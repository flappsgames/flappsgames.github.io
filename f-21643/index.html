TV Darts Show
Description
TV Darts is a skill game unlike other dart games you are likely to play. You must hit the glowing targets on the dart board with your limited amount of darts to move onto the next level, however this is not as easy as it seems. Your hand is constantly moving and the targets always changing, but with great risks come even greater rewards, as you progress through the level your prize fund will increase.
Instructions
Use the mouse to aim your dart. Left click to start the accuracy meter, in the middle for the best shot then click again to throw.
