Close Combat
Description
Close Combat is turn based War Game. There are 2 teams. You control one of them. In each turn you have the choice to either move one of your team members or keep him stationed. Then you have a choice to carry out a particular attack. You can throw a grenade, use a sniping gun or an AK-47 to attack your nearest opponent. Both sides take turns until one team is fully eliminated. You can also play this game with a friend on the same computer.
