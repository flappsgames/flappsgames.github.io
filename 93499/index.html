Description
How would you defend your flag from a waves of enemies? Would you be an engineer with his army of towers?, or a Commando designing attack stategies &&#35;8230; or maybe the destroyer, who leaves nothing in his path? Defend your flag from enemies. Move using arrow keys or WASD. Aim and shoot with mouse. Build a strategy planting towers and earn experience killing enemies. Level up and earn talent points.
