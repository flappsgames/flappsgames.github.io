Squiggle Squid
Description
Squiggle Squid is a mouse-controlled action puzzle game where you control the son of the Kraken as he seeks the legendary Coral Crown. Use Squiggle Squid&&#35;8217;s ink dash to collect plankton and avoid ocean obstacles such as spiny sea urchins. But it won&&#35;8217;t be easy! As the level goals increase, you must master the ink dash to make huge chains, collect power-ups and avoid dangerous sea life. Complete the 50-level Quest Mode or go for the top score in Score Mode.
Instructions
Click away from Squiggle Squid and he will dash and make an ink cloud. Collect plankton when your ink cloud touches them. The farther you dash, the bigger the ink cloud. Quickly dash and make chains for big points.
