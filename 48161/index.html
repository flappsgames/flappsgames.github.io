Gravitex 2
Description
Give your ball just enough power to zoom around orbs in space, grab all the coins, and slip through the portal! This physics-based game contains 100 levels, saved games, and even a level editor! Try making a level with the Level Creator, and leave the code in a comment!
Instructions
UP / DOWN arrow - Change Power LEFT / RIGHT arrow - Change angle SPACE - Fire R - Reset
Developer: kChamp, Category: Puzzle, Distributed by Kongregate
