Pig Wars
Description
Pig Wars is a fun action game where you have to help a little pig to move around the field, throw bombs and blow up as many enemy pigs as possible in each level before the enemy destroys you. The bombs are timed and once you pick them up the countdown begins, throw them towards the enemy pigs before the time runs out. Try to survive for as long as you can!
