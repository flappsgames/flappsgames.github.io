Kyobi
Description
Throw the blocks together to create chains of 3+, and stack up those chains to unlock higher bonuses. The blocks fall relentlessly from above, let them stack too high and it&&#35;8217;s game over. I was inspired by a combination of Match-3 games, the Sega classic Columns and a little bit of Tetris when making this game. Be warned: Hard to play on a laptop / trackpad!
Instructions
Use the mouse. Click and drag the blocks. Release them to throw. Match 3+ of the same color to make them vanish. A Chain Bonus multiplier is awarded if you manage to destroy 3+ blocks quickly enough in succession.
