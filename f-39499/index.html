Mini Golf Islands
Description
Mini Golf Islands is a sports game like no other. You have to hit the golf ball into the hole like many others. However the stunning graphics and unique holes make this game truly stand out from others before it. 20 unique and challenging levels, set on 4 islands await you. Can you finish them all under par?
Instructions
Use the mouse to aim. Click to start the power meter moving, then click again to hit the ball.
