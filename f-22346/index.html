Traffic Mania
Description
The traffic in this city is getting uncontrollable, so we need your help! Guide the traffic coming from different directions on this chaotic intersection in this action-based puzzle game. Use the helicopter for assistance to clean up the streets after an unfortunate break-down. Don't let the cars sit for too long -- they want to get home!
Instructions
Click the icons to move the traffic in a direction, click again to halt traffic. Click the helicopter to pickup broken down vehicles.
