Tuk Tuk City Driving Sim
Description
TUK TUK City Driving Simulator gives you the chance to drive something really cool and unusual round a deserted city. There is something special about these Asian taxis which are starting to be seen in cities all over the world and with no one to tell you off, you can drive it like never before. Try to get the TUK TUK to drift or just drive as fast as you like, but be careful not to roll this three wheeled taxi on its side. If you do crash, don't worry a quick reset will have your TUK TUK Rikshaw all shinny and new again.... Well not really shinny or new, but the dents will be repaired. Ready for you to smash it up again. Don't forget to use the different camera angles to give you either the real behind the wheel feeling or a cool view.
Instructions
Arrows to drive. Space to handbrake
