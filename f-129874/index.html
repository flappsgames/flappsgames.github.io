Air Ambulance Simulator
Description
Air Ambulance Simulator is a unique helicopter flying game. People are in trouble and the only way of reaching them is from the air. Fly through this amazing 3D world and reach your destination. Whether your patient has a broken leg, or a fractured skull.. or even a deadly disease. You can save them all can't you?
Instructions
Mouse click power to turn rotors on/off W/S to increase/decrease altitude A/D to adjust yaw (turn left/right) Hold mouse in direction (on D-Pad) to fly
