Wordspector
Description
Step into the shoes of a genuine Word Detective in this arresting new word puzzler! Solve the Mystery Words with the help of colour coded clues. The less time you take, the more points you'll make! As the game progresses, the words get longer and the time gets shorter. Can you deduce your way through all 15 levels and earn the coveted title of Word Sherlock? Will you be the one to beat on the International Master Detectives Hall of Fame? Either way, a splendid time is guaranteed for all!
Instructions
Use the mouse or the keyboard to input words. Shortcut keys are: Enter = Submit, Space bar = Shuffle, Backspace = Remove letter. Click a letter on the input area to remove it and all following letters. (Given letter can't be removed.) Note: you can retry as many times as you want after losing, but if you make it through the entire game without a single retry, your final score will be DOUBLED! (Quitting the game and continuing later counts as a retry.)
