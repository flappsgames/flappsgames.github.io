Robotic Emergence 2
Description
Humans have apparently been extinct for over a century and robots have taken their place as rulers of the world. But a small group of humans has returned to claim back the Earth! Build your factory city in this challenging strategy game, create robots to fight against the enemy and take over their land to bring Earth once more back to life!
Instructions
Use the mouse to place and upgrade your buildings, towers and boosters.
