Seedz
Description
Evil fruits are attacking, and they're after your pumpkin seeds! Gather your vegetable army and fight the invaders in this funny and challenging defense strategy game! Plant your forces wisely to keep the fruits from stealing all of your pumpkin seeds, upgrade your veggies or sell them to make money and build a more powerful defense!
Instructions
Use the mouse to select your vegetable and plant them in the ground.
