Stunt Rider
Description
In Stunt Rider, you play a retired motorbike stunt rider, and a new upstart who thinks he's better than you has come along to challenge you!! Grab your gear and prove that you're still the best stunt rider of all time. Send him packing to his mama's! All you have to do is click and hold the mouse at the correct time to perfect your jumps and beat the other motorcycle rider. If your opponent gets too far ahead of you it's game over.
Instructions
Hold the left mouse button down to ride downhill, release to launch at the top of the hill.
