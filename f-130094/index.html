Classic Car City Driving Sim
Description
Classic Car City Driving Simulator gives you the chance to drive a beautiful french classic car around a deserted city. Learn what it was like to drive these big, heavy cars with very little grip or grunt. Practice your j-turns or just drive as fast as you like, but be warned, the breaks aren't great and the grip even worst so might find yourself crashing. If you do crash, don't worry a quick reset will have your classic all shiny and new again.... Ready for you to master of driving this lovely classic car. Don't forget to use the different camera angles to give you some really cool views.
Instructions
Arrows to drive. Space to handbrake
