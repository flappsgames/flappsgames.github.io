Golf Cart Parking Challenge
Description
Welcome to the Golf Cart Parking Challenge world! Navigate your way into each of the parking spaces within this beautiful 3D golf course. Watch out for sand traps and the gaps that seem like you can get through.. but you can't. be wary of each obstacle because if you crash... that's back to the driving range for you! Can you reach the green to "putt" your vehicle into the parking spot for a hole in 1? Or... will you fail and be known as " Over Par ". Show the world how good you really are by completing these fantastic levels! - 5* Vehicle Physics - 5* 3D Levels - 5* Gameplay - 5* Challenging and Fun For All Ages!
