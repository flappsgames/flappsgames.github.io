Crash Derby
Description
In Crash Derby you will be put into an arena with other cars. Use the power-up's and traps in game to destroy the other drivers. Be careful as these drivers are no ordinary drivers, they will ram you, use the power-ups inside the arena against you and against other drivers. Avoid the giant blue marbles, Be ready to avoid the shipment of metal that will fall from the skies and watch out for the exploding barrels and the various traps set out around each arena. You choose which power-up you want to use purely by driving the activation pads around the arena’s, Use the power-ups or just ram them into the walls and into the traps… The choice is yours..
Instructions
Use arrows to drive
