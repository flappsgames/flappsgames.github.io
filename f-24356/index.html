Line Racer
Description
Line Racer is a new kind of Racing Game in which you must draw the route that the yellow car will take. Paint the line of the route that you want your car to follow with the mouse. Moving fast to gain speed. You will then start the lap racing against the blue car, cross the line before the other car to win. After each level, you will earn FOG coins which can then be spent on upgrades for your car. Make sure you watch out for the nitrus charging and use it to give your car a quick boost.
Instructions
Use the mouse to draw a line, the car will then follow.
