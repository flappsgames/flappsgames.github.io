Planet Juicer The Core Juice Corporation is counting on you to defend their drill as they mine planets of their cores. The planet cores are used to produce "Core Juice." Do you have the skills needed to defend the drill by building units and researching new technologies? Key Features Nice cartoon graphics Original retro soundtrack 14 In-Game achievements Deep research tree for acquiring new units and upgrades Voice Acting performed for characters / in game speech Mouse Only Controls 3 Worlds World 1 has 10 waves and a big boss World 2 has 15 waves and a boss World 3 has 20 waves and a boss 5 different defence units with 3 upgrade levels Orbital mines with guided lasers Health injection for healing units Defensive walls to block enemies attacks or build units on top of After you complete the game you can continue playing with all of your researched items
