Drift Runners 3D
Description
Fast paced cross-country racing game, with multiple unlocks, upgrades, and zones. Use the handbrake to drift on bends, and build up boost by getting drift chains.
Instructions
Arrow keys or AWSD to drive. CTRL, SHIFT, or Z to use the handbrake. Click the handbrake on corners to start drifting &&#35;8211; and click again when the corner direction changes to increase your drift chain.
