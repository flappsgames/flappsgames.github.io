rotaZion
Description
In rotaZion, you play with a bubble bar that you can't stop rotating ! Collect the bubbles and try to avoid the mines ! Collect the different bonus to protect your bubble bar, makes the mines go slower or destroy all the mines ! Try to beat 100.000 points ;)
Instructions
Move the bubble bar with the arrow keys ! Bubble = 500 Points ! Pixtiz sign = 5000 Points !
