Wings of Genesis
Description
Set in the same universe with the Original Ge.ne.sis game, Wings of Genesis is a side-scrolling shooting game with a heavy touch of RPG influence. Players can pick one of the 3 main characters to begin the adventure and blast through waves of bosses and enemies! Instructions
Your character will follow your mouse. Hold down left mouse button to shoot rapidly. Press the A Key to toggle Auto-Attack Switch weapon with Number 1 or Key Z Use special skill with Number 2 or Key X Use items with Number 3 to 6 or Key C,V,B,N. Pause game with Space Bar.
