cut!
Description
Cutting the yard has never been so fun! Race the clock and cut as much of the yard as you can while avoiding obstacles and attempting to run over Moles.
Instructions
Use the Up, left, and right keys to move. Try to cut the "Target Percentage" of the yard in 25 seconds or less. Avoid cutting flowers and running into dogs. Running over Moles, gas tanks, and wrenches will all give you extra points or a special power up.
