Sim Lemonade 30 Days
Description
Sim Lemonade is a Business Management Game. The aim of the game is to earn as much money as you can by selling lemonade in 30 days. Your progress will depend how good your recipe is, which will be indicated by the various customers emoticons. As you progress you will get used to buying the correct amount of stock depending on the weather and improving your recipe thus improving your popularity and earing you more cash.
