Prizma Puzzle Challenges
Description
Prizma Puzzle Challenges is a new tile-based puzzler with the smooth and addicting gameplay, the fabulously 3D rendered graphics and excellent music. Try to complete all 8 zones with 40 levels and a lot of achievements!
Instructions
Use the mouse to click on a start node. Click on contiguous nodes to create your path. To complete a round, you must hit finishing node without crossing your path or running out of moves.
