Army Bike 3D
Description
Army Bike 3D is a dirt bike game. Ride your bike through multiple levels held in different army base's whilst doing your best to jump over several army territorial equipment performing crazy stunts along the way. The challenge is to keep control of your bike, judge your speed and time otherwise it's game over for you.
Instructions
Up - Accelerate Down - Brake Left/Right - Lean left/right Space - Jump
