Clarence&apos;s Big Chance
Description
Clarence has never been on a date in his 35 years of life. But today that changes! He must run and jump around four large levels, collecting items to impress the lady of his dreams while going about his daily routine! How well you perform determines which of the five endings you will get!
Instructions
LEFT and RIGHT ARROWS: Move DOWN ARROW: Crouch UP ARROW: Read signs, use doors SPACE: Jump ENTER: Show the menu ESC: Return to title screen M: Toggle mute
