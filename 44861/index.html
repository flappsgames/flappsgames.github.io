Egg Defender
Description
Survive long enough for your egg to hatch into a fuzzy little polar bear snack. Four unlockable game modes Fifteen achievements Five alternate endings.
Instructions
Use the arrow keys to move. Press SPACE to slide on your belly. Tips: - Slide into other penguins to knock them off the edge. - The polar bear won't leave until he's eaten his fill, unless you bug him with your beak. - Don't let your guard down, there are a few surprises lurking in the water. - Once you've got the hang of things, you can sit on your egg in the nest to advance the level timer more quickly. Have fun :D
