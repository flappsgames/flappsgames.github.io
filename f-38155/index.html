New York Taxi License 3D
Description
New York Taxi License 3D is Driving Game. You must complete various driving based tasks to earn your New York Taxi License. There are 18 levels and some of them are on icy roads. You must avoid traffic violations like going through red lights or hitting the curbs. There are features like taking tourists around sight seeing and driving backward on an obstacle filled road.
Instructions
Use the arrow keys to drive around.
