Blood Bath Bay
Description
Oh no! Zombies can now swim and breathe underwater! You are the last hope against the zombie outbreak; scientists believe that the only cure can be found under the sea - a rare kind of snail. It is up to you to find them and save mankind in this action-packed, deep-sea thriller of a game. Collect snails, kill zombies and medicinal fish to recover your health.
