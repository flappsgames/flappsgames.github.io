School Bus License 3
Description
School Bus License 3 is a American Scool Bus Driving Simulator Game. There are 17 levels, they range from picking children up from a variety of bus stops and dropping them at school. Avoiding road blocks and make sure the ride is smooth enough so the kids are not sick on the bus. The faster you complete each task the better your score will be, so it's worth going back to replay a level and improve your score.
Instructions
Use the arrow keys to drive around and spacebar for handbrake.
