Paper Floods 2
Description
Save even more dudes from drowning in this challenging sequel to the original Paper Floods! Clear the flood-stricken people's path in order for them to float back to the surface and catch some air! Can you solve the intricate puzzles with only the few objects you have at your disposal? Test your logic skills with this challenging and addictive doodle game!
Instructions
Use the mouse to move your pieces and left click to place them
