Youda Legend The Curse of the Amsterdam Diamond
Description
Youda Legend: The Curse Of The Amsterdam Diamond is a Hidden Object Puzzle Game. The goal of the game is to find all of the items on the list within the scene, so that you may continue on your adventure. If you are having trouble finding items, you can use your special compass to help you find difficult objects, but only if you have allowed it to charge up. Later on in the game, you must use the items you find to solve the mystery of The Amsterdam Diamond, so make sure you find everything!
