Galaxy Golf HD
Description
Galaxy Golf is a free 3D fun fantasy packed mini golf game set in space and packed with amazingly challenging levels. You start with some basic golf shots to get the hang of the game but move on to more challenging mini golf holes. Layered with traps throughout the levels, be careful or you'll be going from a par 3 to 10 shots over! - Fantastic Levels. - Stunning Graphics. - Easy to Use Controls.
Instructions
Click and drag to aim. Click on power bar to set power and putt
