Taxi City Driving Sim
Description
Taxi City Driving Simulator gives you the chance to drive a Taxi around a deserted city. There is no police to slow you down and no one to pick up. Practice your jumping skills or just drive as fast as you like, but be careful not to damage the paintwork as you haven't finished paying for it yet. If you do crash, don't worry a quick reset will have your truck all shiny and new again.... Ready for you to smash it up again. Don't forget to use the different camera angles to give you cool views and different angles.
Instructions
Arrows to drive. Space to handbrake
