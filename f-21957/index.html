Off Roaders 3D
Description
Off Roaders 3D is an intense, off-road Jeep racing game. Hugging tight turns on the slippery mud, guide your truck to the finish line! Speeding is great, but it'll take some skill to master the harsh course -- let off on the gas a bit to successfully pass the bends (and your opponents!). Compete online for the best times!
Instructions
Arrow keys to drive
